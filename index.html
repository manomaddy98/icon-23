About The UNIVERSITY

Aiming to bring about change and process through education, the Tmt. Kannammal Educational Trust was constituted in 1985 and started and started at Thai Moogambigai Polytechnic College. Within three years of its inception, Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute was founded in 1988, and the Thai Moogambigai Dental College was started in the year 1991. Both the colleges acquired Deemed University status in 2003 as Dr. M.G.R Educational and Research Institute as per the order of the University Grants Commission, New Delhi, and the Ministry of Human Resources and Development, Government of India, New Delhi. The Institution is accredited by NAAC with an ‘A+’ Grade (University with Graded Autonomy)

About The DEPARTMENT

The Department of Information Technology was started in the year 2001 and offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs. In this era of digital world, the IT department offers an excellent atmosphere for students to learn and acquire the necessary skills as demanded by the industry.
The department is enriched with experienced faculty members, well-equipped laboratories, a department library, and R&D facilities. Students and faculty members are part of professional societies like IEEE, IETE, CSI, CISCO, etc., in order to gain contemporary knowledge about the happenings in the industry.
The department has established strong links with

industrial and academic experts, both within and outside the country.Besides high-quality teaching, various webinars, FDPs, Conferences, Symposiums, and Workshops are organized frequently both at the national and international levels.
Our department faculty members have a remarkable number of publications. Our enthusiastic students take up several real-time projects each year. Also aspiring students are given international opportunities through student exchange programs, internships, and summer training programs. Students who graduated in our department are placed as system developers, architects, technical leaders, and managers in many multinational companies.

ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

×
  • E-Governance strives to bridge the digital divide, ensuring that all citizens have equal access to Government services. This inclusivity promotes social equity and broadens the reach of public services. Leveraging data analytics and artificial intelligence, E-Governance enables informed decision-making. Analyzing data trends can lead to more effective policy formulation, resource allocation, and overall governance. The digitalization of government processes promotes transparency by making information readily accessible to the public. This transparency, coupled with robust cybersecurity measures, enhances accountability and trust in governmental institutions.
  • E-Governance platforms facilitate direct and real-time engagement between citizens and the government. Through online portals, social media, and mobile applications, citizens can actively participate in governance, providing feedback and contributing to decision-making processes. Automation of routine administrative tasks reduces bureaucratic delays, streamlines processes, and minimizes paperwork. This is not only enhancing overall efficiency but also contributes to cost savings in the long run. As digital governance expands, ensuring the security of sensitive data and protecting citizens’ privacy become paramount. Robust cybersecurity measures must be in place to safeguard against potential threats.
  • The ongoing evolution of E-Governance is reshaping the way government interacts with citizens. By embracing technology, fostering inclusivity, and ensuring transparency, we are collectively laying the foundation for a more connected, efficient, and responsive future in governance.
